I am working in a personal project calledIsTheWeb.

I am developing and incorporating new plugings and modules on top of Laravel`s CMS OctoberCMS to make easy to create and manage any tech project.
Among others:

  • IsCoorporate: An easy and intuitive way to manage all relevant information of a business; employees, projects, services, images galleries, documents, clients... all in a single place.
  • IsCommerce: E-commerce platform with products, categories, features, filters, credit card and PayPal payments system. Orders system, shipping and delivery and stock...
  • IsMarketing:  The platform has components that facilitate the integration of Google Analytics to track content and subscribers mailing, the Open Graph protocol to communicate with social networks, Mailing Lists through Mailgun API...
